Introduction
Welcome to Idle mIne:Reincarnated! This is a remix of the original game Idle Mine aswell as a remix of Idle Mine:Remix! This game is not complete, but updates are on their way! Get started by clicking the big ore on the left of the screen.

Pickaxe creating
Upon breaking any ore, there is a 5% (can be increased) chance to get a gem. Using those gems you can create better pickaxes. However, there is no guarentee that the new pickaxe will be better than the previous one.

Enchancenments
After creating a dud, ou get a potential enchancenment, that will be transferred onto your pickaxe when you do create a worthy one. Each enchancenment gives a 20% (compouding) boost to your damage.

Factory & Miners
The factory is simple. The first miner generates XP, eventually leveling up your factory, giving you boosts. The second miner generates the first miner at 0.01x speed. The third one generates the second one, etc.
